Questions?

Quick answers

Do people need an app to scan my card?

No. Any phone camera scans the QR and opens your profile instantly in the browser. Saving your contact is one tap — no downloads, no sign-ups for them.

What happens if I change my number?

Just edit your card in the dashboard. The QR points to your live profile, so every card you've already printed or shared updates automatically. You never reprint.

How do MoMo payments work?

You connect your own Paystack payment link. When someone taps Pay on your card, they pay with MoMo, card, or bank — and the money goes directly to your own account. HaloCard never holds your money.

Do I need to print anything?

No — your card link works everywhere: WhatsApp status, Instagram bio, TikTok, email signature. Printing is optional, and if you do, exports are 4K print-shop quality. You can also write your card to an NFC tap card.

Is the free plan really free?

Yes — one full card with your kente profile, contact saving, and all your socials, free forever. Upgrade only when you want lead capture, payments, and analytics.
